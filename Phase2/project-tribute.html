Michael Jackson

  • 1958: Born on August 29 in Gary, Indiana, U.S., to Katherine and Joseph Jackson. He was the eighth of ten children.
  • 1962: Sings with his brothers in the Jackson 5
  • 1969: The Jackson 5 signs with Motown
  • 1971: Begins recording solo
  • 1972: "Ben" is his first number one solo hit
  • 1978: Plays the Scarecrow in The Wiz
  • 1979: Releases Off the Wall, which sells millions of copies
  • 1982: Releases Thriller, which sells more than 40 million copies and becomes the best-selling album of all time
  • 1984: Jackson's face is burned during a Pepsi ad shoot
  • 1985: Buys ATV Music, which owns the rights to John Lennon and Paul McCartney songs
  • 1987: Releases Bad, which sells 26 million copies
  • 1991: Releases Dangerous, which sells 7 million copies
  • 1993: Jackson is accused of child molestation by a 13-year-old boy
  • 1994: Marries Lisa Marie Presley, daughter of Elvis Presley
  • 1996: Jackson marries Debbie Rowe, a nurse, and they have two children, Prince and Paris
  • 2003: Jackson is arrested and charged with child molestation
  • 2005: Jackson is acquitted after a 14-week trial
  • June 25, 2009: Jackson dies in Los Angeles at the age of 50 from acute propofol intoxication
